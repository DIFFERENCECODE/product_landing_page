## 9 · ⚠️ Compliance Safe Language Guide

### Phrases to Avoid

| 🚫 Never say | ✅ Say instead |
|---|---|
| "Diagnose heart disease" | "Monitor lipid patterns associated with cardiovascular trends" |
| "Prevent diabetes" | "Track metabolic signals related to glucose sensitivity" |
| "Treat high cholesterol" | "Monitor cholesterol trends over time" |
| "Cure your metabolism" | "Understand and track your metabolic patterns" |
| "Clinically proven to lower cholesterol" | "Part of a lifestyle approach informed by peer-reviewed research" |
| "FDA-approved" *(unless literally true)* | "CE-marked", "clinical-grade instrument" (where verified) |
| "Replaces your doctor" | "Complements regular medical care" |
| "Reverse heart disease" | "Observe trends in cardiovascular-relevant biomarkers" |
| "Guaranteed results" | "30-day money-back on the device" |
| "Medical-grade AI" | "AI-powered insight tool" (informational, not medical) |
| "Lowers your biological age" | "Your Biological Age Score may reflect changes in your tracked biomarkers" |

### Approved Framing

- "**Monitoring and insight tool** — not a medical device for diagnosis."
- "Meo does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any disease."
- "Consult a healthcare professional before making changes based on your readings."
- "Results are for informational purposes and are not a substitute for medical advice."
- "Trends are most meaningful when built over many readings."
- "Meo AI surfaces patterns — you and your doctor decide what to act on."

### Required Disclaimers (place in footer + checkout)

1. **Footer / all pages:**
   > *Meo is a wellness and monitoring tool. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult your GP or a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice. Results are not a substitute for professional testing.*

2. **Checkout page, before "Pay":**
   > *By ordering, you acknowledge that Meo provides informational insights based on lipid readings and is not a medical device for diagnosis or treatment.*

3. **In-app at every Meo AI interaction:**
   > *Meo AI gives pattern-based insight, not medical advice. If something concerns you, speak to a healthcare professional.*

### Marketing-Safe "Biological Age" Language

Never imply Biological Age Score reflects total health, life expectancy, or clinical reality. Always frame as:

- "A **score** based on your tracked lipid patterns"
- "Observational, not predictive"
- "**Your** baseline — not a population benchmark"
- "Updates as your tracked biomarkers change"
